Registration

How do I register ? + -

We strive to make things simple for you and so is registration. You can register using your mail ID, mobile number. You can either register by clicking ‘sign in’ on top right of home page or at the time of checkout.

Are there any charges for registration ? + -

Registration is free of charge.

Do I have to necessarily register to shop on GrocerMax ? + -

It is not compulsory to register to shop on GrocerMax. You can login and buy as a guest as well. But we strongly recommend you to register. This will enable us to design personalized offers which we feel will be of relevance and interest to you.

Can I have multiple registrations ? + -

Every unique mail ID entitles you to one registration. We encourage you to have one active registered account as it will keep all your past transactions, shopping lists, loyalty points in one place and easy to encash.

How much are the delivery charges ? + -

Grocermax.com offers free delivery for orders above Rs. 250/-. A nominal delivery fee of Rs. 50/- will be levied for orders below this amount.

Do you deliver in my area ? + -

Currently, Grocermax delivers in Gurgaon, Dwarka, Palam, Vikaspuri, Janakpuri, Punjabi Bagh only. Multi city expansion is on the cards and we will be covering all of Delhi NCR soon.

Will someone inform me if my order delivery gets delayed ? + -

Absolutely! We will call and inform you if your order is going to reach after the delivery time specified by you.

What is the minimum order for delivery ? + -

There is no minimum order at Grocermax.com. We will, however, levy a nominal fee of Rs. 50/- for orders below Rs. 250/-. Above this amount, home delivery is absolutely free.

Do you do same day delivery ? + -

Yes. If you place your order before 12 Noon, we will be able to deliver on the same day.

Is Same Day Delivery applicable to only a few products or all products ? + -

Same day delivery applies to every single product available on our website. Life just got so much better!

What if I receive an item which is damaged/expired/not in usable condition ? + -

We have a ‘no question asked’ returns policy in case of returns. At any point before taking the delivery, you feel that the product has not been delivered to you in a desired state, please hand it back to our delivery team. In case, you have already paid – the balance amount will be refunded to you in the same debit card / credit card / bank account that you used to make the payment in 10 working days. If it was up to us, we would do it faster but the reversal procedures by banks take that much time.

What is refund process in case of cancellation ? + -

In case you cancel your order the money will be refunded back in your debit/credit card within 7-10 working days.

Will I always get the freebies mentioned in your offer/s? + -

You'll always get the freebies that are mentioned in the offers except for bill busters (such as Bade Miyan, Fortune Combo & others). A bill buster is many products bundled into one offer with certain discounts and/or freebies. In case of bill busters the freebies are valid for 3 times per customer.
